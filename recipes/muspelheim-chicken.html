Muspelheim Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken of the Fire Giants: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Flames of Muspelheim: 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Surtur’s Fiery Rub: 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika, 1 teaspoon of chili powder, 1 teaspoon of cumin, 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1 teaspoon of onion powder, 1 teaspoon of black pepper, 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • Lava Juice: Juice of 1 lime
  • Embers of the Forge: 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Molten Butter: 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Ashes of Muspelheim: Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Igniting the Flames: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Brush the chicken breasts with the Flames of Muspelheim (olive oil) on both sides.
  2. Enveloping in Surtur’s Rub: In a small bowl, mix together Surtur’s Fiery Rub. Rub this spice mixture generously over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are well-coated.
  3. Dousing with Lava Juice: Squeeze the Lava Juice (lime juice) over the seasoned chicken breasts.
  4. Whispering Embers: In a skillet, melt the Molten Butter over medium heat. Add the Embers of the Forge (minced garlic) and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Grilling in the Inferno: Place the chicken breasts on the grill and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through and have a nice charred exterior.
  6. Finishing TouchesRemove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with the Ashes of Muspelheim (chopped cilantro).
