Helheim Cod

Ingredients

  • Cod of the Forgotten Souls: 4 fillets of fresh cod
  • Mist of Niflheim: 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Hel’s Blackened Spice1 tablespoon of paprika, 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1 teaspoon of onion powder, 1 teaspoon of thyme, 1 teaspoon of oregano, 1 teaspoon of black pepper, 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • Frost Giant’s Lemon: Juice of 1 lemon
  • Shadows of the Underworld: 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Ethereal Butter: 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Veil of Helheim: Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Steps

  • Summoning the Mist: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Brush the cod fillets with the Mist of Niflheim (olive oil) on both sides.
  • Enchanting the Cod: In a small bowl, mix together Hel’s Blackened Spice. Rub this spice mixture generously over the cod fillets, ensuring they are well-coated.
  • Casting the Frost Giant’s Blessing: Squeeze the Frost Giant’s Lemon (lemon juice) over the seasoned cod fillets.
  • Whispering Shadows: In a skillet, melt the Ethereal Butter over medium heat. Add the Shadows of the Underworld (minced garlic) and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  • Embracing the Flames: Place the cod fillets in the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they develop a nice, dark crust.
  • Journey to Helheim: Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the cod is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  • Unveiling the Veil: Remove the cod from the oven and garnish with the Veil of Helheim (chopped parsley).
